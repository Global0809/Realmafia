From the heart of the party
to the soul of the booth.

Sexy Mafia is an Ibiza nightlife veteran turned sonic architect. After 25 years at the epicenter of club culture, he now commands the booth with "Grid Style"—a sophisticated, high-energy fusion built for dancefloors from Dubai to Rome.

For a quarter of a century, Sexy Mafia has been a living archive of Ibiza's nightlife. He didn't just observe the evolution of dance music; he lived it—from the center of the floor, in the world's most legendary clubs. This unique trajectory, from definitive reveler to authoritative DJ, provides an unparalleled "insider" lens.

He understands the ecosystem of a perfect night because he helped build it. Now, he channels that veteran's energy into meticulous curation and production. His sound is the synthesis of a life lived in rhythm: sophisticated in its structure, raw in its emotion, and visceral in its impact.

"Sexy Mafia doesn't just play music; he transmits the frequency of the island that forged him."
